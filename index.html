Unspoken: Poems of Love and Memory

A collection of poems written in silence, shaped by memory

Prologues

Poetry captures and preserves the moments time cannot erase. It echoes the words we carry in silence but never quite speak. Through poems, we weave our stories, our memories, and our most hidden truths.

Here you'll discover the emotions that are preserved in words—love, loss, longing, and memory that continue to live beyond silence.

About

"Unspoken: Poems of Love and Memory" is a collection born from silence, shaped by feeling. These poems explore love, loss, longing, and transformation.

They are not written to explain life. They are written to feel it, to remember it, to hold what time tries to take away.
