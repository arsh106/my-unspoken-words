Prologues

Poetry captures and preserves the moments time cannot erase. It echoes the words we carry in silence but never quite speak. Through poems, we weave our stories, our memories, and our most hidden truths.

Here you'll discover the emotions that are preserved in words—love, loss, longing, and memory that continue to live beyond silence.

About

Unspoken: Poems of Love and Memory is a collection of original poems inspired by lived emotions, reflection, and imagination.

These poems explore the many dimensions of the human experience—love, memory, childhood, friendship, identity, hope, grief, resilience, healing, dreams, and the quiet emotions that often remain unspoken.
