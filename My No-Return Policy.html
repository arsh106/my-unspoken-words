Unspoken: Poems of Love and Memory

My "No-Return" Policy

Head held high, fueled by extreme ego,
my nature is defined by this ruthless duo.
I live by a strict “no-return” policy.
If my self-respect feels even slightly damaged,
call it an extreme form of psychology.

Once I desert something or someone,
the entire canvas turns barren, a desert.
That moment becomes the point of no return,
and “revert” has no place in my dictionary.
